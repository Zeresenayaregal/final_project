About Me

Greetings! I'm Zeresenay Yaregal, originally from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Growing up, I attended "Science Shared Campus," an environment brimming with like-minded individuals where programming was revered as a cornerstone of education. This early exposure ignited my passion for technology and inspired me to delve deeper into the world of software engineering.

Currently, I'm on an exciting journey of learning and growth. I'm enrolled in the Software Engineering program at ALX, where I'm honing my skills and knowledge in this dynamic field. Additionally, I'm diving into the intricacies of full-stack development through Directed, further expanding my expertise and capabilities.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I'm a multifaceted individual with diverse interests and hobbies. I'm an avid reader, particularly drawn to non-fiction books, especially those in the realm of self-help and personal development. In my spare time, I channel my creativity into producing beats using FL Studio, immersing myself in the rhythmic world of music production. And when I'm not lost in the melodies, you'll often find me on the football field, indulging in my love for the beautiful game of soccer.

Driven by curiosity and a thirst for knowledge, I'm constantly seeking new experiences and challenges that allow me to grow personally and professionally. With a blend of technical acumen and creative flair, I'm excited to continue exploring the vast possibilities that the world of technology has to offer.
